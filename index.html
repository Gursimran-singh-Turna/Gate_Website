ELIGIBILITY

GATE Eligibility Criteria 2021 - IIT Bombay has announced the relaxation in GATE 2021 eligibility criteria due to covid-19 situation. As per the announcement, candidates in their pre-final year of undergraduate degree will also be eligible for GATE 2021. The detailed GATE 2021 eligibility criteria will be released in information brochure along with the application form. The GATE eligibility criteria consist of parameters like age limit, academic qualification, nationality and more which candidates will have to meet in order to appear for the entrance examination. Also, two new papers - Environmental Science and Engineering (ES) and Humanities and Social Science (XH) and students from arts and humanities background will also be eligible to appear in GATE 2021 for XH paper.
Latest: GATE 2021 registration will be open from September 14 to 30, 2020. GATE 2021 exam dates announced, exam to be held on February 5 to 7 and 12 to 14.
Candidates have to fulfil the GATE eligibility criteria 2021 failing which, they will not be allowed to appear for the national level engineering postgraduate entrance test. Therefore, candidates are suggested to refer to the eligibility criteria before proceeding towards filling the GATE 2021 application form. There is no age limit to apply for GATE. Candidates can appear for the entrance exam any number of times.
Eligibility criteria of GATE 2021 states that candidates who possess certification from any of the professional societies must ensure that those examinations conducted by the societies are approved by MHRD/AICTE/UGC/UPSC as equivalent to B.E./B.Tech./B.Arch./B.Planning etc. Also, candidates who registered for certification equivalent to B.E./B.Tech from any of the professional societies will also be eligible for GATE 2021. Candidates must note that these eligibility criteria are just for GATE examination, criteria for admissions into M.Tech courses at different IITs will be decided separately.
